Military Service

From an early age, I knew that I wanted to serve my country, and when that opportunity presented itself, I knew I wanted to go above and beyond. Out of 400 applicants, I was one of 12 selected to start the medic training program to become a Civil Affairs operator. This path was long, extremely difficult, and filled with complex issues that needed to be resolved with strict deadlines. After a full year of medical training, I graduated with honors, and joined my first 4 man team in January of 2013. On this team, I learned the necessity of team work as well as the importance of strong attention to detail, and built friendships that will last a lifetime.
