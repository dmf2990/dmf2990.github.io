Military Service

From an early age, I knew that I wanted to serve my country, and when that opportunity presented itself, I wanted to go above and beyond the average Soldier. Out of 400 applicants, I was one of 12 selected to undergo the Civil Affairs Medical Training Program (CAMs).

After nearly a year of rigorous training, I graduated from the CAMs course second in my class, and joined my first 4 man team in January of 2013. This experience was vital in shaping my adulthood, and it allowed me to understand the strength in combining a strong work ethic with team work, punctuality, and attention to detail. My military service was a very special time in my life, but unfortunately cut short by injury.
